What You Should Know

When you use drugs, they hijack your brain's reward system—the same system that responds to things that make you feel good naturally. They flood your brain with dopamine, creating an artificial high that your brain starts to crave. But here's the thing: you are stronger than any chemical. Your mind and body have incredible resilience, and understanding how these substances work is your first step toward freedom.
